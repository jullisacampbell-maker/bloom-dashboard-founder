Troubleshooting

Common Issues

My data disappeared. Bloom OS saves to your browser's local storage. Clearing browser data or switching browsers will reset it. Consider copying important notes.

The app feels slow. Try refreshing the page. If issues persist, clear cache and reload.

My plan didn't unlock. If you paid but your plan isn't showing, check your email for a Stripe receipt and contact support with your email address.

Dark mode won't save. Make sure your browser allows local storage for this site (not in private/incognito mode).
